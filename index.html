About CryptoMondays Fort Lauderdale

CryptoMondays is a global movement for people who live and breathe crypto — the ones who want to learn, build, connect, hire, and innovate in Web3.

Our Fort Lauderdale chapter brings together passionate builders, investors, creators, and the crypto-curious to share ideas and make real connections.

  • Meetups with lightning talks, demos, and open networking
  • A welcoming community — newcomers and veterans alike
  • Practical insights, opportunities, and collabs

Born in New York City, CryptoMondays now spans 40+ cities worldwide — including Saigon, Los Angeles, Tel Aviv, Paris, London, Miami, San Juan, Singapore, San Francisco, Zurich, Denver, and Istanbul — all united by one mission: to use crypto and blockchain to make the world a better place.
